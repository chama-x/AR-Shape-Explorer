### Overview
Shape Explorer AR makes learning geometry easy. You can place 3D shapes in your room to see how they look and understand their size and scale.

### Core Features
- **Shape Library**: Includes Cube, Sphere, Cone, Cylinder, and Torus.
- **Simple Controls**: Easily rotate and scale shapes with your fingers.
- **Math Info**: Shows Volume and Surface Area formulas in real time.
- **Clear Design**: Realistic lighting and colors for better visibility.

### Tech Stack
- **Library**: Three.js
- **AR Pipeline**: WebXR and Variant Launch 
- **User Interface**: Fast and responsive CSS design
